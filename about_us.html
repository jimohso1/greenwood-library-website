About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to the Greenwood Library, a vibrant community hub dedicated to fostering knowledge, connection, and growth. We are more than just a place for books; we are a cornerstone of learning and discovery for readers, thinkers, and dreamers of all ages in the Greenwood community.Our mission is to provide free and open access to a diverse collection of resources, from classic literature to modern digital media, while offering a welcoming space for exploration and engagement. Whether you're seeking a quiet corner to read, a place to connect with others, or access to educational programs, the Greenwood Library is here to inspire and support you. Founded with a passion for literacy and lifelong learning, our library is staffed by dedicated professionals who are committed to empowering our community through knowledge. We offer a range of services, including reading programs, technology access, community events, and educational workshops, all designed to enrich lives and spark curiosity. At Greenwood Library, we believe in the power of stories, ideas, and shared experiences to bring people together. Join us in celebrating the joy of learning and building a stronger, more connected community.
